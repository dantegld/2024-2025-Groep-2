Retourbeleid van MyShoes

At MyShoes we strive to make sure you are completely satisfied with your purchase. If the product does not meet your expectations, such as the wrong color, size or other preferences, we offer you the opportunity to return the product within 3 days of receipt.

Returns are possible under the following conditions:

  • Return period: You have 3 days to notify us that you want to return the product, starting from the date of receipt of your order. This can be done easily by sending an email to our customer service via
    myshoes@gmail.com.

  • Returns Policy: The product must be in its original condition, unused and undamaged. All original labels and packaging must be present. The product must not show any signs of use.

  • Cash payout: If you return the product within 30 days of the return notification, you will receive your purchase amount back. The refund will be processed via the same payment method that you used for the purchase.

  • Return procedure: After submitting your return request, you will receive a confirmation from us with further instructions on how to return the product. Make sure you get a proof of postage when you return the package. This is important for your own administration and may be necessary in case of any disputes. The shipping costs for returns are at your own expense, unless the product is defective or we made a mistake with your order..

  • Exceptions: Personalised or customised products cannot be returned unless they are defective or do not meet the agreed specifications.

  • Questions or problems: Do you have any questions about your return or are you experiencing problems? Please feel free to contact our customer service via myshoes@gmail.com or 0499875432. We are happy to help you.

At MyShoes we value your satisfaction and want to make sure you feel good when shopping with us. Thank you for your trust in MyShoes!
